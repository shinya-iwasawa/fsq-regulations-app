Regulation 30. Food packaged in polyvinyl chloride container shall not contain excess vinyl
chloride monomer.
No person shall import, prepare or advertise for sale or sell any food in any rigid or semi-rigid
package, appliance, container or vessel made of polyvinyl chloride if the food contains more than 0.05
mg/kg of vinyl chloride monomer.
