Regulation 1. Citation, commencement and application.
(1) These Regulations may be cited as the Food Regulations 1985.
(2) These Regulations shall come into force on such date as the Minister may appoint by
notification in the Gazette.
(3) These Regulations shall not apply to any food prepared, produced or packaged for export
outside Malaysia.
