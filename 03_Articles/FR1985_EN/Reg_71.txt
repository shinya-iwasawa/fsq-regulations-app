R egulation 72. Rye bread.
Rye bread shall be the bread obtained by baking a yeast – leavened dough prepared from rye flour
t o which may be added not more than 70 per cent of wheat flour.
R egulation 73. Wheat-germ bread.
Wheat – germ bread shall be the bread obtained by baking a yeast – leavened dough prepared from
wheat – germ meal, water, and –
(a) wheat flour;
(b) wholemeal wheat flour; or
(c) a mixture of wheat flour and wholemeal wheat flour, in proportions of not less than 5 kg
of wheat – germ meal to 100 kg of wheat flour or of wholemeal wheat flour or of the
m ixture, as the case may be.
