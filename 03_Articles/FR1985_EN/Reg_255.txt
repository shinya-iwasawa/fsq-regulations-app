Regulation 255. Coconut cream powder.
(1) Coconut cream powder shall be the soluble white powder made from coconut cream as
specified in regulation 254. (1) Subs. P.U.(A) 306/2009:24]
(2) Coconut cream powder –
Updated until December 2023
(a) shall not contain more than –
(i) 3 per cent of water; and
(ii) 2.5 per cent ash; and
(b) shall contain not less than –
(i) 50 per cent of fat; and
(ii) 5 per cent of protein derived from coconut.
(iii) Coconut cream powder may contain permitted food conditioner and permitted
antioxidant.
[Am. P.U.(A) 162/88]
R egulation 256. Dessicated coconut.
(1) Dessicated coconut shall be the dried and shredded kernel of the fruit of Cocos nucifera.
(2) Dessicated coconut shall not contain more than 3 per cent of water.
